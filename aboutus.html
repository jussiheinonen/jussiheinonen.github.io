Team Lingora®

Team Lingora® = Berries United Oy + Noooner LIMITED

Team Lingora® is a collaboration between Berries United Oy and Noooner LIMITED. Berries United Oy is a Finnish company that produces Lingora® and holds the patent for it, and Noooner LIMITED is a UK company that distributes Lingora® Natural Oral Health products in the UK and Ireland.

Dr Pirjo Pärnänen - inventor, Berries United

The inventor of Lingora®, Dr Pirjo Pärnänen (Associate Professor in Oral Microbiology, DMD, PhD) has conducted laboratory studies of fermented lingonberry juice for over 15 years, as well as numerous clinical studies.

Results of studies are outstanding.
Fermented lingonberry juice reduces dental caries-related Streptococcus mutans and Candida yeast counts, and is designed to aid oral home care routines.





Ville Heinonen - CEO, Berries United

Ville Heinonen is the CEO and the founder of Berries United. He is an experienced entrepreneur specialising in natural wellbeing products based on wild berries such as the sea buckthorn, and more recently the lingonberry. His love for these bright berries has enabled him to create a healthy product range that not only refreshes your taste buds, but has health benefits also.

He has now partnered with Dr Pirjo Pärnänen to promote Lingora® Natural Oral Health products in Finland and globally.

Samantha Phillips - Marketing Manager, Noooner LIMITED

Samantha Phillips has a background in marketing and corporate communications. She's passionate about all-natural products and continuous innovation that happens in the natural health products space. She's an excellent communicator and is fluent in Dutch, English and German.






Jussi Heinonen - CEO, Noooner LIMITED

Jussi Heinonen is the CEO of Noooner LIMITED. He is an experienced entrepreneur specialising in digital marketing and information systems. He has a passion for creating and promoting innovative products and services.






Dan Barron - Head of Sales, Noooner LIMITED
