Team Lingora®

Team Lingora® = Berries United Oy + Noooner LIMITED

Team Lingora® is a collaboration between Berries United Oy and Noooner LIMITED. Berries United Oy is a Finnish company that produces Lingora® and holds the patent for it, and Noooner LIMITED is a UK company that distributes Lingora® Natural Oral Health products in the UK and Ireland.

Dr Pirjo Pärnänen - Head of R&D, Berries United

Dr Pirjo Pärnänen (Associate Professor in Oral Microbiology, DMD, PhD) is the inventor of the Lingora® and co-founder of Berries United.

Her pioneering and award-winning product is the result of over 15 years of tireless research, testing and clinical trials. Lingora® Natural Oral Rinse is a ground-breaking product in the world of oral health.

Dr Pärnänen is a highly respected expert in the field of oral health and has published numerous scientific articles on the subject. She is also a sought-after speaker at international conferences and events.



Ville Heinonen - CEO, Berries United

Ville Heinonen is an experienced and charismatic entrepreneur specialising in natural wellbeing products based on wild berries such as the sea buckthorn, and more recently the lingonberry.

Ville’s passion for wild arctic lingonberries has enabled him to create an oral health product range that not only tastes good, but has significant oral health benefits also.

Berries United manufactures Lingora® Natural Oral Health products in Finland for global distribution.

Samantha Phillips - Marketing Manager, Noooner LIMITED

Samantha’s professional background lies in multilingual Customer Services and Corporate Training.

As part of Noooner LIMITED, she moved to the speciality of Marketing.

However, as the business is still young, she turns her hand to everything from logistics, fulfilment, website design, trade show management and PR.



Jussi Heinonen - CEO, Noooner LIMITED

Jussi is an experienced and multi-talented entrepreneur specialising in digital marketing and information systems.

His background in software, media and telecom industries lead him to work for companies such as the Financial Times.

At the beginning of 2021 he turned his hand to independent consultancy and started his own company, Noooner LIMITED.



Dan Barron - Head of Sales, Noooner LIMITED

Dan Barron has a background in sales and business development.

He's highly experienced in products focusing in FMCG, Food Supplements and Medical Devices.

Dan is an expert in managing field sales, telesales and national account management.
