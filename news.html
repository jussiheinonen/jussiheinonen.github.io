September 29

A new research paper by Dr Pärnänen published in the Microorganism Journal

Recently published article by Dr Pärnänen and her peers in the Microorganism Journal discusses the causes of dry mouth and how this exposes the oral microbiome to changes, such as yeast infection, and how the Fermented Lingonberry Juice (Lingora® Natural Oral Rinse) enhances maintenance of oral hygiene and contributes to the control of plaque.

August 29

Lingonberry harvest season is here

It's that time of the year again when the wild arctic lingonberries are ripe and ready to be picked.
It has been estimated that the average annual yield in Finland is 257 million kg but only 10% of the annual lingonberry harvest is picked.
source: arcticlingonberry.fi

We are looking forward to the new season and the fresh berries that will be used as the main ingredient in Lingora® Natural Oral Rinse and the Spray.

May 31

Fermented Lingonberry Mouthwash is Vegan Approved

We are delighted to announce that our Fermented Lingonberry Mouthwash has been approved by the Vegetarian Society as vegan.
The Vegetarian Society’s vegan trademark is the most widely recognised vegan label in the UK.
We are proud to have met the strict criteria set by the Vegetarian Society and to be able to display the vegan trademark on our product.
