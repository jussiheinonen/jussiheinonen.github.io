DELIVERY PIPELINES
Speed, Transparency, Visibility

Well-designed Delivery Pipelines increase productivity and makes work more enjoyable for product delivery team. Automation is a key characteristic of a Delivery Pipeline. It provides speed and increases the output by removing repetition of manual steps.

Typical Delivery Pipeline consists of multiple phases (or steps) that are executed in a sequential order. Each phase of a pipeline performs a specific task. When task is completed successfully, it triggers the next phase the pipeline automatically.

SPEED

Speed is king.
Moving faster than competitors, creating prototypes and adjusting to changes in operational environment. Quicker the response to feature requests, security threats and bug reports results in happier customers.

TRANSPARENCY

Auditing and accountability improves the quality. What changed, when was the change made, and by whom?

VISIBILITY

Overseeing the current state of play of production estate. Making it accessible for widest possible audience. Monitoring availability, measuring performance with metrics and aggregating log events.
